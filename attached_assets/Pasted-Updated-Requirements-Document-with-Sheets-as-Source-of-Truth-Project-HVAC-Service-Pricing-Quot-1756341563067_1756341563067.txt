Updated Requirements Document (with Sheets as Source of Truth)

Project: HVAC Service Pricing & Quoting App
Client: Giesbrecht HVAC (GHVAC)

1. Clear Objective

Create a mobile-first quoting app for HVAC technicians that:

Starts simple (no login/password at first).

Lets techs generate quotes on-site immediately.

Pulls all pricing and settings directly from Google Sheets (no in-app settings).

Allows adding new/custom parts and consumables.

Generates copyable text quotes (for FieldEdge or messaging).

Sends email notifications of each quote to manager/admin.

Automates Trello card creation for follow-up and ordering.

2. MVP – Phase 1 (Simple Calculator & Quote Generator)
Access & Simplicity

No password/login for now.

Direct link, easy for techs to use in the field.

Quote Inputs

Customer info: name only.

Part categories:

Populated directly from Google Sheets pricing guide.

Includes live pricing updates for parts and consumables.

Custom part entry:

Part number.

Availability.

Warranty status.

Vendor-sourced price.

Quote Outputs

Generated text block with all part info, totals, and description.

Copy button → paste directly into FieldEdge.

Email copy → automatically sent to manager/admin.

Workflow Integration

If quote accepted → Trello card under “Parts to be Ordered.”

If quote pending → Trello card under technician’s “Follow-up” list.

3. Phase 2 – Conditional Logic & Smart Features
Conditional Part Logic

Example: If condenser replacement is selected → automatically add refrigerant.

Rules for coils, compressors, etc. that require extra materials.

Warranty / Loyalty Pricing

Checkbox: “Installed by GHVAC?”

If yes → prorated labor pricing (1–10 years since install).

Pulled from warranty logic defined in Google Sheets.

Smart AI Summary (Technician Notes)

Voice input preferred → speech-to-text capture.

AI-generated bullet point summary of repair/job plan.

Stored alongside quote for admin review and price book updates.

4. Notifications & Admin

Quote notifications: email to admin/manager for every new quote.

Technician tracking: dropdown with tech names.

History log: all quotes saved in app (no login required).

Backend Source of Truth:

All markup, profit margin, labor rate, refrigerant $/lb, etc. are maintained in Google Sheets.

App simply fetches values live from the sheet.

No separate in-app settings menu needed.

5. Long-Term (Phase 3 – Sales Funnel & Expansion)

Automated follow-up system (SMS/email reminders).

Dashboard/reports: weekly review of quotes, accepted jobs, pending jobs.

CRM-lite functions: customer info, history, and pipeline view.
